Weekend Activities

Based on popular demand, we've blocked off rooms for Wednesday through Saturday. Stay as long as you'd like, but please do not feel obligated to stay for the entirety. For details on booking your stay, see here.

Important note: Menemsha is a dry town. That means you should consider purchasing any beverages that you'd like to consume throughout the weekend—with the exception of the hosted events—before you arrive.

Thursday:

Take a stroll into Menemsha Village. Grab some fresh seafood or coffee at one of the local eateries or spend the afternoon at Menemsha beach.

Grab a bike and go explore the island. There are some wonderful walking trails all around Menemsha

Lucy Vincent Beach is a picturesque state park with a sandy beach. It's a 15m bike ride away. Look here we Google Mapped it for you. Or checkout these other local beaches. The inns have walk on and parking passes to each.
